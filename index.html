About me

I'm a blockchain research engineer at Zama working on privacy preserving smart contracts using fully homomorphic encryption.

I also study theoretical computer science at Université de Montréal under the supervision of Prof. Abdelhakim Senhaji Hafid.

Research interests: design, analysis and implementation of blockchain systems; succinctly verifiable computation; cryptography.
